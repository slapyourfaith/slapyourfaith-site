About Slap Your Faith

Slap Your Faith exists to help men and families put their faith back at the center of their homes. We build simple, practical tools that make it easier to pray together, lead with purpose, and build a house that actually stands when life hits hard.

Support the Mission

If these tools and messages impact you, consider supporting the work. Every gift helps us keep the Prayer Circle and Family Blueprint free to use while we keep building better tools for families who want to follow Jesus with boldness and order.
